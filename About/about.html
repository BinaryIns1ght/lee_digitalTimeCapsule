Welcome to my About Me page...

My name is Donovan S. Lee. I am currently a 22-year-old Korean-American, living in California. I used to live within the Bay Area. With the recent moving, I now live within the outskirts of the Bay Area. I am currently a Visual Communication Design major.

I currently seek to improve upon my current designing skills. Despite my few experiences in designing, I am a long way from finally making my designs stand out drastically and even possibly add on to the pre-existing aesthetic.

I fall in the line of Generation Z, yet the many things I've enjoyed are nowadays often considered as "old-fashioned" or antique. The many items/media I love are:

  • Old Games, like "Super Mario Brothers", or "Sonic The Hedgehog"
  • "Happy Together" by the Turtles
  • "I'm Blue" by Eiffel 65
  • Frutiger Aero aesthetic
  • Frutiger Aurora aesthetic
