It's kind of hard to consider what is good design for many people. Many see pieces with a deeper meaning behind it is a good design. Some see visually pleasing pieces and see those as good designs.

For me, I see good design is where someone is willing to put in good effort in making the piece and has some creative element to it. Some pieces may be incredibly beautiful, while others can have a more Avante Garde to their designs. It's quite a difficult thing to claim something as well designed.

Some pieces have a tone of anger, stress, or gloom. Another can have a sense of optimism, glee, and bubbly. What is considered as good design to be is a design that has a good amount of effort put onto it and allows the viewer to understand and explore the piece.
